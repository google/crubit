# Cardi, the Crubit Mascot

Meet **Cardi**, the official mascot of the Crubit project!

![Cardi](images/cardi.svg)

## Who is Cardi?

Cardi is a friendly blue squid.

## Lore

As a squid, Cardi is naturally at home in the deep ocean. But in the world of
software, **Cardi lives under the C** (well, C++).

Cardi's mission is to help bridge the gap between C++ and Rust, ensuring they
can communicate smoothly.
